About

At PB 23, we're passionate about architecture and strive to create buildings that catalyze the public good. We believe that thoughtful design can transform the way people live, work, and play, and have a positive impact on the physical, economic, cultural, and social health of cities. From small urban interventions to large-scale urban developments, we look at each project with the goal of improving our communities.

Our mission at PB 23 is to bring you the most comprehensive, global perspective on architecture, interiors and urban design. Our work begins with a focus on the projects that top design experts are talking about right now. We cover editorial architecture news, new developments, and building designs at the forefront of their field by leading firms from around the world.

Problems

PB-23 did not had way to present themselves on the internet. They needed a way to show their clients the projects they had done and their values and mission to clients so they understand and choose them for their next project. So, we needed to create a repsonsive UI for website of PB-23 Drywall.

Goals

The UI should be formal and conservative to ensure the credibility and professionalism to the clients but should also present the company as a creative by showing it on the website. The website needed to be responsive so that it could be accesible on any device. Also client should be able to communicate through the website with the company an let them know what they want.
