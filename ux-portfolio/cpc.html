Process

Problems

CPC had a website that showcased their projects, their company info, their contact info and other information. Although it represented the company but it lacked professionalism, aesthetics and consistency. The website was not something that one could call responsive.
Here's a snapshot of the website before redesign

Goals

The UI should be formal and conservative to ensure the credibility and professionalism to the clients but should also present the company as a creative identity. The website needed to be responsive so that it could be accesible on any device. Also client should be able to communicate through the website with the company an let them know what they want.

Information Architecture

To define the Information Architecture of the website I first created a detailed content inventory of original website (not shown here). Then did the competitive analysis and used card sorting to create a new IA for the website. Though I ended up with almost the same sitemap and navigation.
